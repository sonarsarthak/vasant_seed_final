About Vasant Seeds

Our journey of growth and commitment to premium onion seeds

Our Story

Founded in 2000, Vasant Seeds began with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide farmers with the highest quality onion seeds while promoting sustainable agricultural practices. What started as a small seed distribution company has grown into one of India's leading onion seed producers.

Over two decades, we have expanded our operations across multiple states, established research facilities, and built strong relationships with farmers across the country. Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us a trusted name in the agricultural sector, particularly in onion seed production.
